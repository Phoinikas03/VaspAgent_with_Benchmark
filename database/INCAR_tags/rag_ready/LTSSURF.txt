LTSSURF = .TRUE. | .FALSE. Default: LTSSURF = .FALSE. Description: if LTSSURF set to .TRUE., the standard parametrization of the Tkatchenko-Scheffler method is replaced by the one designed to enable reliable modeling of structure and stability for a broad class of organic molecules adsorbed on metal surfaces is activated  .
