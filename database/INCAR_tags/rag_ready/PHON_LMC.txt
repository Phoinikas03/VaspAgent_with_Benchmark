PHON_LMC =  Default: PHON_LMC = .FALSE. Description: This flag controls whether electron-phonon interactions from Monte-Carlo sampling are calculated or not. This feature is only available from VASP version 6.0 or higher. For further usage of this tag see: Electron-phonon interactions from Monte-Carlo sampling .
