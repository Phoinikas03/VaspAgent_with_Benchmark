LUSE_VDW =  Default: LUSE_VDW = .FALSE. Description: LUSE_VDW =.TRUE. switches on the use of a nonlocal vdW-DF functional. These functionals depend on the electron density at two points in space and model long-range van der Waals (dispersion) correlation effects.
