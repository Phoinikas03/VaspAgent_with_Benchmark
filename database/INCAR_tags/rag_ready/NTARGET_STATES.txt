NTARGET_STATES =  Description: Controls which Wannier states are excluded in constrained random phase approximation. Check also NCRPA_BANDS . This tag is effective for ALGO =CRPA and ignored otherwise. For instance selects the Wannier state 1, 2 and 4, where the ordering of the Wannier states depends on the chosen basis set.
