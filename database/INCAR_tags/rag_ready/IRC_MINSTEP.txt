IRC_MINSTEP = real Default: IRC_MINSTEP = 0.0250 Description: Lower limit for the step size (in fs). The damped-velocity-Verlet algorithm for the IRC calculations uses an adaptively varying size of the time step. It depends on the estimated accuracy of the previous step. IRC_MINSTEP defines the lower limit for the step size in fs. Related tags and articles IRC calculations , IRC_DIRECTION , IRC_STOP , IRC_DELTA0 , IRC_MAXSTEP , IRC_VNORM0
