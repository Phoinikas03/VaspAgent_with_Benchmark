IVDW_NL =  Description: IVDW_NL allows to select the kernel of the nonlocal van der Waals part of a functional (available as of VASP.6.4.0). IVDW_NL =1 corresponds to the kernel of Dion et al.  and IVDW_NL =2 to the kernel rVV10  . Note that the kernel of Dion et al. contains one adjustable parameter ( ZAB_VDW ), while the rVV10 kernel contains two such parameters ( BPARAM and CPARAM ).
