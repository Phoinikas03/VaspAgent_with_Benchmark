IRC_MAXSTEP = real Default: IRC_MAXSTEP = 3.000 Description: Upper limit for the step size (in fs). The damped-velocity-Verlet algorithm for the IRC calculations uses an adaptively varying size of the time step. It depends on the estimated accuracy of the previous step. IRC_MAXSTEP defines the upper limit for the step size in fs. Related tags and articles IRC calculations , IRC_DIRECTION , IRC_STOP , IRC_DELTA0 , IRC_MINSTEP , IRC_VNORM0
