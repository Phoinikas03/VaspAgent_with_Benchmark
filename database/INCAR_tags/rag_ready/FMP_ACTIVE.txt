FMP_ACTIVE = logical (aray) Description: Select which atom types in the POSCAR -file participate in swapping within the Müller-Plathe method . FMP_ACTIVE specifies whether or not (.TRUE. or .FALSE., respectively) an atomic type allowed for swapping within the Müller-Plathe method . One item for each of the atomic types defined in POSCAR must be supplied. Related tags and articles Müller-Plathe method , FMP_DIRECTION , FMP_SNUMBER , FMP_SWAPNUM , FMP_PERIOD
