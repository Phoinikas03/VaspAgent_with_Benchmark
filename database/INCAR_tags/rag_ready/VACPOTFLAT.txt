VACPOTFLAT =  Default: VACPOTFLAT = 0.1 Description: Maximum permissible 2D-averaged electric field for a region considered to be field-free in eV/Angstrom. A region of space is considered to be field-free if the 2D-averaged electric field ( LVACPOTAV =True) is smaller than VACPOTFLAT .
