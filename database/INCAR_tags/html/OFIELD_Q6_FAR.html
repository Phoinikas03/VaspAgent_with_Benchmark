    OFIELD_Q6_FAR = [real] 

    Description: OFIELD_Q6_FAR sets the far fading distance for the computation of a continuous Q 6 {\displaystyle Q_6} parameter in the interface-pinning method.

    The unit of OFIELD_Q6_FAR is Å {\displaystyle \AA} .
