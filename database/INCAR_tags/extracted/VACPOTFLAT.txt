<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">VACPOTFLAT</a> = [real]<br/> Default: <b>VACPOTFLAT</b> = 0.1 
</p>
<p>Description: Maximum permissible 2D-averaged electric field for a region considered to be field-free in eV/Å.
</p>
<p>A region of space is considered to be field-free if the 2D-averaged electric field (<a href="/wiki/index.php/LVACPOTAV" title="LVACPOTAV">LVACPOTAV</a>=True) is smaller than <a class="mw-selflink selflink">VACPOTFLAT</a>.
</p>
