<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">QSPIRAL</a> = [real array]<br/> Default: <b>QSPIRAL</b> = 3*0.0

<p>Description: the <a class="mw-selflink selflink">QSPIRAL</a>-tag specifies the spin spiral propagation vector.
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">QSPIRAL</a> specifies the spin spiral propagation vector in direct coordinates of the reciprocal lattice.
</p>
