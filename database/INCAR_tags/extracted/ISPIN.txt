<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ISPIN</a> = 1 | 2<br/> Default: <b>ISPIN</b> = 1 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ISPIN</a> specifies spin polarization.
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ISPIN</a>=1: non-spin-polarized calculations are performed.</li>
<li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ISPIN</a>=2: spin-polarized calculations (collinear) are performed.</li></ul>
<p>By combining <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ISPIN</a> with <a href="/wiki/index.php/MAGMOM" title="MAGMOM">MAGMOM</a> collinear magnetism can be studied.
</p>
