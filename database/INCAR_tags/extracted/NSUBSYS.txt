<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">NSUBSYS</a> = [integer array] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NSUBSYS</a> defines the atomic subsystems in calculations with multiple Anderson thermostats (in case VASP was compiled with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/Precompiler_flags" title="Precompiler flags">-Dtbdyn</a>).
</p>
<p>Up to three user-defined atomic subsystems may be coupled with independent Andersen thermostats<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Andersen80_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Andersen80-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/index.php/MDALGO" title="MDALGO">MDALGO</a>=13).
</p>
<p>These subsystems are defined by specifying the last atom for each subsystem (two or three values must be supplied). For instance, if total of 20 atoms is defined in the <a href="/wiki/index.php/POSCAR" title="POSCAR">POSCAR</a>-file, and the initial 10 atoms belong to the subsystem 1, the next 7 atoms to the subsystem 2, and the last 3 atoms to the subsystem 3, <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NSUBSYS</a> should be defined as follows:
