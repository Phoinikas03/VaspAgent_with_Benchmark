<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">KERNEL_TRUNCATION/LTRUNCATE</a> truncates the coulomb kernel to remove electrostatic interactions along non-periodic dimensions
</p>
<p>Setting <a class="mw-selflink selflink">KERNEL_TRUNCATION/LTRUNCATE</a> = T  switches on coulomb kernel truncation methods. These methods removes interactions with periodic replicas in non-periodic directions, i.e. along the surface normal for 2D materials and along all directions for 0D systems such as atoms and molecules.
</p>
