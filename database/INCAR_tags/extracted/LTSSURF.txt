<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LTSSURF</a> = .TRUE. | .FALSE.<br/> Default: <b>LTSSURF</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: if <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LTSSURF</a>  set to .TRUE., the standard parametrization of the Tkatchenko-Scheffler method is replaced by the one designed to enable reliable modeling of structure and stability for a broad class of organic molecules adsorbed on metal surfaces is activated<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ruiz:prb:2016_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ruiz:prb:2016-1">[1]</a></sup>.
