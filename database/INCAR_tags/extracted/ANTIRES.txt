<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ANTIRES</a> = 0 | 1 | 2<br/> Default: <b>ANTIRES</b> = 0 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ANTIRES</a> determines whether the Tamm-Dancoff approximation is used or not. 
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ANTIRES</a>=0 Tamm-Dancoff approximation (TDA)</li>
<li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ANTIRES</a>=1 yields exact results at ω=0 at roughly the same cost  as TDA</li>
<li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ANTIRES</a>=2 beyond Tamm-Dancoff, coupling between positive and negative frequencies</li></ul>
<p>VASP uses the procedures outlined in reference <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Sander_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Sander-1">[1]</a></sup> to include contributions beyond TDA. Beyond-TDA calculations increase the computational time and memory requirements by typically a factor of 2.
</p>
