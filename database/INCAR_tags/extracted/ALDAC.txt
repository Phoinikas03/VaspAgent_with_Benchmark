<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ALDAC</a> = [real] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ALDAC</a> is a parameter that multiplies the LDA correlation functional or the LDA part of the GGA correlation functional. 
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ALDAC</a> can be used as the fraction of LDA correlation in a Hartree-Fock/DFT hybrid functional. 
</p>
