<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">IBAND</a> = [integer array]<br/> Default: <b>IBAND</b> = not set 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">IBAND</a> sets a list of bands that contribute to calculating the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/Band-decomposed_charge_densities" title="Band-decomposed charge densities">partial charge density</a>.
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">IBAND</a> selects a subset of bands for which the partial charge density is calculated when <a href="/wiki/index.php/LPARD" title="LPARD">LPARD</a> = .TRUE..
Partial charge densities are written to the <a href="/wiki/index.php/PARCHG" title="PARCHG">PARCHG</a> file, or one of its variants, depending on the setting of <a href="/wiki/index.php/LSEPB" title="LSEPB">LSEPB</a> and <a href="/wiki/index.php/LSEPK" title="LSEPK">LSEPK</a>.
</p>
<p>E.g. if
<code>IBAND = 20 21 22 23 45</code>
the charge density will be calculated for the four bands 20 to 23 and band 45, and <a href="/wiki/index.php/NBMOD" title="NBMOD">NBMOD</a> will be set to 5.
</p>
