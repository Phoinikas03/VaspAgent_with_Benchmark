<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">NTARGET_STATES</a> = [integer array] 
</p>
<p>Description: Controls which Wannier states are excluded in constrained random phase approximation. Check also <a href="/wiki/index.php/NCRPA_BANDS" title="NCRPA BANDS">NCRPA_BANDS</a>.
</p>
<p>This tag is effective for <a href="/wiki/index.php/ALGO" title="ALGO">ALGO</a>=CRPA and ignored otherwise.
For instance 
</p>
<p>selects the Wannier state 1, 2 and 4, where the ordering of the Wannier states depends on the chosen basis set.
