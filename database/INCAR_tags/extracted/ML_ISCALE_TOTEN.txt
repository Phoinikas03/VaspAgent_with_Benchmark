<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_ISCALE_TOTEN</a> = [integer]<br/> Default: <b>ML_ISCALE_TOTEN</b> = 2 
</p>
<p>Description: This tag specifies how to scale the energy data in the machine learning force field method.
</p>
<p>The following cases are possible:
</p>
<ul><li><code style="padding: 2px"><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_ISCALE_TOTEN</a> = 1</code>: The total energy is scaled to the total energy of the isolated atoms given by <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_EATOM_REF" title="ML EATOM REF">ML_EATOM_REF</a>.</li>
<li><code style="padding: 2px"><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_ISCALE_TOTEN</a> = 2</code>: The total energy is scaled to the average of the training data. This is the default setting.</li></ul>
