<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">DQ</a> = [real]<br/> Default: <b>DQ</b> =  0.001 
</p>
<p>Description: step size for the finite difference <i>k</i>-space derivative in the linear response calculation of chemical shifts.
</p>
<p>Typical values for <a class="mw-selflink selflink">DQ</a> are in the range [0.001 - 0.003]. The default is often sufficient.
</p>
