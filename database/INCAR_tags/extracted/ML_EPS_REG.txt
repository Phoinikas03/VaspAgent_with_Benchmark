</p>
<p>If at any point during iterating the above equations, the quadratic norm of errors (eight column of <code>REGR/REGRF</code> in <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_LOGFILE" title="ML LOGFILE">ML_LOGFILE</a>) becomes too large (more than 1.2 times larger than in previous iterations), the code assumes that numerical issues
(loss of significance) have occurred, and  then <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_EPS_REG</a> is automatically doubled. Furthermore, if the regression does not converge within 10 steps, <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_EPS_REG</a> is also increased by a factor of 4. The maximum allowed iteration depths is 50 (the iteration number is the second entry of <code>REGR/REGRF</code> in <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_LOGFILE" title="ML LOGFILE">ML_LOGFILE</a>). When 50 iterations are reached, no force field is created and there is most likely something seriously wrong in the calculation.
</p>
