<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LNABLA</a> = .TRUE. | .FALSE.<br/> Default: <b>LNABLA</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LNABLA</a>=.TRUE. evaluates the transversal expression for the frequency dependent dielectric matrix.
</p>
<p>Usually VASP uses the <a href="/wiki/index.php/LOPTICS#epsilon" title="LOPTICS">longitudinal expression for the frequency dependent dielectric matrix</a>.
It is however possible to switch to the computationally somewhat simpler transversal expressions by selecting <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LNABLA</a>=.TRUE. (Eqs. 17 and 20 in Ref.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gajdos:prb:06_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-gajdos:prb:06-1">[1]</a></sup>).

</p>
<p>Except for the purpose of testing, there is however hardly ever a reason
to use the transversal expression, since it is less accurate.
</p>
