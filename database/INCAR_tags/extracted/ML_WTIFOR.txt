<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_WTIFOR</a> = [real] 
</p>
<p>Description: This tag sets the weight for the scaling of the forces in the training data within the machine learning force field method.
</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_IWEIGHT" title="ML IWEIGHT">ML_IWEIGHT</a>, <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_WTOTEN" title="ML WTOTEN">ML_WTOTEN</a>, <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_WTIFOR</a>, <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_WTSIF" title="ML WTSIF">ML_WTSIF</a> form a group of tags which set the normalization and weighting of ab initio training data, i.e.  energies, forces and stresses of the training structures. The main control tag is <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_IWEIGHT" title="ML IWEIGHT">ML_IWEIGHT</a>, please also have a look at its detailed description. If <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_IWEIGHT" title="ML IWEIGHT">ML_IWEIGHT</a>=1 the weight has unit eV/Angstrom and is used to divide the data by it. For <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_IWEIGHT" title="ML IWEIGHT">ML_IWEIGHT</a>=2 and 3 the weights are unitless and multiplicative.
</p>
