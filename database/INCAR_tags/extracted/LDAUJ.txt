<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LDAUJ</a> = [real array]<br/> Default: <b>LDAUJ</b> = NTYP*0.0 
</p>
<p>Description: Sets the effective on-site exchange interactions (eV).
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LDAUJ</a> specifies the strength of the effective on-site exchange interactions in eV. It must hold one value for each atomic species.
</p>
