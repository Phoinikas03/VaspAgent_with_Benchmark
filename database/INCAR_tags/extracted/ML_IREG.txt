<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_IREG</a> = [integer]<br/> Default: <b>ML_IREG</b> = 2 
</p>
<p>Description: This tag specifies whether the regularization parameters are kept constant or not in the machine learning force field method.
</p>
<p>The following cases are possible for this tag:
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_IREG</a>=1: The (initial) precision (<a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_SIGV0" title="ML SIGV0">ML_SIGV0</a>) and noise (<a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_SIGW0" title="ML SIGW0">ML_SIGW0</a>) parameters are kept constant.</li>
<li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_IREG</a>=2: The parameters are optimized (default).</li></ul>
