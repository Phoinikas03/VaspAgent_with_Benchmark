<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LVGVAPPL</a> = .TRUE. | .FALSE.<br/> Default: <b>LVGVAPPL</b> =  .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LVGVAPPL</a> determines whether the <i>vGv</i> orbital magnetic susceptibility is applied in the calculation of the CSA tensor.
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LVGVAPPL</a> is available as of VASP.6.4.0.
</p>
