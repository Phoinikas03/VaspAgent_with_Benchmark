<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ML_LEATOM</a> = [logical]<br/> Default: <b>ML_LEATOM</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: This term specifies whether the total atomic energy is written out or not. 
</p>
<p>This tag specifies whether the total atomic energy (potential energy plus kinetic energy) for each MD step is written out to the  <a href="/wiki/index.php/ML_EATOM" title="ML EATOM">ML_EATOM</a> file. It also writes the atomic positions and lattice parameters at every MD step to this file.
</p>
