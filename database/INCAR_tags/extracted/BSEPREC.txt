<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEPREC</a> = Low | Medium | High |  Accurate<br/> Default: <b>BSEPREC</b> =  Medium 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEPREC</a> determines the precision of the time-evolution algorithm for solving the Bethe-Salpeter or Casida equations, i.e., the timestep and the number of steps. 
</p>
<p>The timestep in the time-evolution calculation is inversely proportional to the maximum transition energy <a href="/wiki/index.php/OMEGAMAX" title="OMEGAMAX">OMEGAMAX</a> and the number of steps is inversely proportional to the broadening <a href="/wiki/index.php/CSHIFT" title="CSHIFT">CSHIFT</a>. Depending on the <a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEPREC</a>  stable these parameters are scaled depending on the precision tag <a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEPREC</a>.
</p>
