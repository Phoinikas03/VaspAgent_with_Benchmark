<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">HILLS_W</a> specifies the width of the Gaussian hill (in units of the corresponding collective variable) used in metadynamics (in case VASP was compiled with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/Precompiler_flags" title="Precompiler flags">-Dtbdyn</a>).
</p>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/index.php/Metadynamics" title="Metadynamics">metadynamics</a> (<a href="/wiki/index.php/MDALGO" title="MDALGO">MDALGO</a>=11 | 21), the bias potential is given as 
</p>
