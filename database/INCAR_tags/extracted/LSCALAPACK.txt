<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSCALAPACK</a> = [logical] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSCALAPACK</a> controls the use of scaLAPACK. 
</p>
<p>For <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSCALAPACK</a>=.TRUE., VASP uses scaLAPACK routines for the orthonormalization of the wave functions and subspace diagonalizations.
</p>
<p>The use of scaLAPACK for the LU decomposition in the orthonormalization of the wave functions may be independently switched off (<a href="/wiki/index.php/LSCALU" title="LSCALU">LSCALU</a>=.FALSE.). 
</p>
