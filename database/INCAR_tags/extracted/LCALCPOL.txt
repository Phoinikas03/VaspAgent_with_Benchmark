<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LCALCPOL</a> = .TRUE. | .FALSE.<br/> Default: <b>LCALCPOL</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LCALCPOL</a>=.TRUE.  switches on the evaluation of the Berry phase expressions for the macroscopic electronic polarization in accordance with the so-called <a href="/wiki/index.php/Berry_phases_and_finite_electric_fields#Modern_Theory_of_Polarization" title="Berry phases and finite electric fields">Modern Theory of Polarization</a>.
</p>
<p>For <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LCALCPOL</a>=.TRUE., VASP calculates the electronic contribution to the polarization, along the three reciprocal lattice vectors <b>G</b><sub>i</sub>, i=1,2,3,  (i.e. Σ<sub>i</sub> <b>P</b>·<b>G</b><sub>i</sub>) in a single run (unlike <a href="/wiki/index.php/LBERRY" title="LBERRY">LBERRY</a>=.TRUE.).
</p>
