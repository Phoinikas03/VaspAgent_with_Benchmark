<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ESTOP</a> = [real] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ESTOP</a> specifies the stop condition for stochastic MP2.
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink"><span style="color:black">ESTOP</span></a> defines the energy accuracy in units of eV for each individual tau-point of the two individual MP2 energy contributions (direct MP2 term + exchange MP2 term). Since the statistical errors of each contribution is independent, the standard deviation of the MP2 energy can be estimated as
