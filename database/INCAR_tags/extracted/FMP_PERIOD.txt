<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">FMP_PERIOD</a> = integer<br/> Default: <b>FMP_PERIOD</b> =  10 
</p>
<p>Description: Number of time steps between two swapping events in the <a href="/wiki/index.php/M%C3%BCller-Plathe_method" title="Müller-Plathe method">Müller-Plathe method</a>.
</p>
<p>This tag defines how many MD steps are done between two consecutive velocity-swapping events. The period is counted in MD steps and not in simulation time.
</p>
