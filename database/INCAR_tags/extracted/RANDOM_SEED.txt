<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">RANDOM_SEED</a> = [integer][integer][integer]<br/> Default: <b>RANDOM_SEED</b> = based on the system clock 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">RANDOM_SEED</a> specifies the seed of the random-number generator (compile VASP with <a href="/wiki/index.php/Precompiler_options#-Dtbdyn" title="Precompiler options">-Dtbdyn</a>). 
</p>
<p>The random-number generator (RNG) generates a sequence of random numbers, which is initialized by the tag <a class="mw-selflink selflink">RANDOM_SEED</a>.
For example, in molecular dynamics simulations, the RNG can be used to initialize atomic velocities. Hence, the seed for the RNG influences the trajectory of a molecular dynamics simulation.
