<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">QMAXFOCKAE</a> = [real array] 
</p>
<p>Description: Controls at which wave vectors the local augmentation 
charges are fitted to obtain an accurate charge augmentation on the plane-wave grid. 
</p>
<p>We do not recommend setting these tags manually, except after careful inspection of the VASP code (fast_aug.F). The default values are 6.0 Å<sup>-1</sup> 
if <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/NMAXFOCKAE" title="NMAXFOCKAE">NMAXFOCKAE</a>=1 (corresponding to 140 eV), and 5.0 and 10 Å<sup>-1</sup> (corresponding to 95 eV and 380 eV) for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/NMAXFOCKAE" title="NMAXFOCKAE">NMAXFOCKAE</a>=2.
</p>
