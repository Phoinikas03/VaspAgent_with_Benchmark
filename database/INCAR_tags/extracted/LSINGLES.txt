<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSINGLES</a> = .TRUE. | .FALSE.<br/> Default: <b>LSINGLES</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: Switch on singles contribution to correlation energy for <a href="/wiki/index.php/Practical_guide_to_GW_calculations#Low_scaling_GW_algorithms" title="Practical guide to GW calculations">GW algorithms</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-klimes:jcp:143_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-klimes:jcp:143-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSINGLES</a> enables the calculation of the singles contributions to the correlation energy that can be represented by the following Feynman (time-ordered) diagrams:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kaltak:thesis2015_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-kaltak:thesis2015-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-klimes:jcp:143_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-klimes:jcp:143-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<p><span typeof="mw:File"><a class="mw-file-description" href="/wiki/index.php/File:SinglesDiagrams.png"><img decoding="async" height="97" src="/wiki/images/thumb/e/eb/SinglesDiagrams.png/320px-SinglesDiagrams.png" srcset="/wiki/images/thumb/e/eb/SinglesDiagrams.png/480px-SinglesDiagrams.png 1.5x, /wiki/images/thumb/e/eb/SinglesDiagrams.png/640px-SinglesDiagrams.png 2x" width="320"/></a></span>
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LSINGLES</a> is used in combination with the <a href="/wiki/index.php/ACFDT/RPA_calculations#Low-scaling_ACFDT.2FRPA_algorithm" title="ACFDT/RPA calculations">low-scaling ACFDT/RPA</a> and <a href="/wiki/index.php/Practical_guide_to_GW_calculations#Low_scaling_GW_algorithms" title="Practical guide to GW calculations">GW</a> algorithms.  
</p>
<p>If the <a href="/wiki/index.php/ACFDT/RPA_calculations#All-in-one_approach_to_calculate_ACFDT-RPA_total_energy" title="ACFDT/RPA calculations">ACFDT/RPA algorithm</a> is selected with <a href="/wiki/index.php/ALGO" title="ALGO">ALGO</a>=RPAR|ACFDTR and <a class="mw-selflink selflink"><span style="color:black">LSINGLES</span></a> is set, the code calculates two singles contributions and writes following lines to <a href="/wiki/index.php/OUTCAR" title="OUTCAR">OUTCAR</a>
</p>
<p>Here, <b>renomalized HF singles</b> corresponds to the renormalized singles contribution suggested by Ren and coworkers:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ren:prb:88_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-ren:prb:88-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
