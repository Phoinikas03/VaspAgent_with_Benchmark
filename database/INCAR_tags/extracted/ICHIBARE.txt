<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ICHIBARE</a> =  1 | 2 | 3<br/> Default: <b>ICHIBARE</b> =  1 
</p>
<p>Description: determines the order of the finite difference stencil used to calculate the magnetic susceptibility.
</p>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">ICHIBARE</a> specifies the order of the finite difference stencil used to calculate the magnetic susceptibility (second order derivative in Eq. 47 of Yates <i>et al.</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-yates:prb:2007_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-yates:prb:2007-1">[1]</a></sup>). <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ICHIBARE</a> may be set to 1, 2, or 3. Often the default (<a class="mw-selflink selflink">ICHIBARE</a>=1) is sufficient. A higher <a class="mw-selflink selflink">ICHIBARE</a> results in a substantial increase of the computational load.
</p>
