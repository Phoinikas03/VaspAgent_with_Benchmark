<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> = [real]<br/> Default: <b>EDIFFG</b> = <a href="/wiki/index.php/EDIFF" title="EDIFF">EDIFF</a>×10 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> defines the break condition for the ionic relaxation loop.
</p>
<p>When <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> is positive, the relaxation is stopped when the change of the total energy is smaller than <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> between two ionic steps.
</p>
<p>When <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> is negative, the relaxation is stopped when the norms of all the forces are smaller than |<a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a>|. This is usually a more convenient setting.
</p>
<p>If <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EDIFFG</a> = 0, the ionic relaxation is stopped after <a href="/wiki/index.php/NSW" title="NSW">NSW</a> steps.
</p>
