<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEELECTRON</a> = [real array] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEELECTRON</a> sets the coordinates of the fixed electron of the exciton wavefunction
</p>
<p>If <a class="mw-selflink selflink">BSEELECTRON</a> is set in a BSE calculation, VASP computes exciton wavefunction for the first <a href="/wiki/index.php/NBSEEIG" title="NBSEEIG">NBSEEIG</a> states.
The coordinates are provided in direct (fractional) coordinates.
</p>
<p>When fixing the position of the particle, ensure that it is not fixed exactly at the center of an atom or coincides with a node of the wavefunction. To avoid that, shift the fixed coordinate slightly away from the center of the atom.
