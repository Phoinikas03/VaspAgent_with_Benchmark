<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Available_functionals">Available functionals</span></h2>
<p>This table lists the LDA and GGA functionals available in VASP. The names of functionals which end with "_X" and "_C" correspond to exchange-only and correlation functionals, respectively. 
</p>
<p><span style="color:blue">(1)</span> The Slater LDA exchange includes relativistic effects.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-macdonald:jpc:1979_27-0"><a href="#cite_note-macdonald:jpc:1979-27">[27]</a></sup>
</p>
<p><span style="color:blue">(2)</span> The exchange component was designed in particular to be used as the exchange component of <a href="/wiki/index.php/Nonlocal_vdW-DF_functionals" title="Nonlocal vdW-DF functionals">Nonlocal vdW-DF functionals</a> and with <a href="/wiki/index.php/AGGAC" title="AGGAC">AGGAC</a>=0 such that only LDA is used for the local correlation, see <a href="/wiki/index.php/Nonlocal_vdW-DF_functionals#List_of_nonlocal_vdW-DF_functionals" title="Nonlocal vdW-DF functionals">list of nonlocal vdW-DF functionals</a>.
</p>
