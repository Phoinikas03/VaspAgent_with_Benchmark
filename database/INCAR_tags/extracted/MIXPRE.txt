<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a> = 0 | 1 | 2 | 3<br/> Default: <b>MIXPRE</b> = 1 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a> specifies the metric in the Broyden mixing scheme(<a href="/wiki/index.php/IMIX" title="IMIX">IMIX</a>=4).
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a>=0</li></ul>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a>=1</li></ul>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a>=2</li></ul>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a>=3 (implemented for test purposes; <b>not</b> recommended)</li></ul>
<p>The preconditioning is done only on the total charge density (i.e. up+down component) and not on the magnetization charge density (i.e. up-down component). In our experience, the introduction of a metric always improves the convergence speed. The best choice is <a class="mw-selflink selflink">MIXPRE</a>=1 (i.e. the default).
</p>
