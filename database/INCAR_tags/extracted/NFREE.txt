<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">NFREE</a> = [integer] 
</p>
<p>Description: depending on <a href="/wiki/index.php/IBRION" title="IBRION">IBRION</a>, <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NFREE</a> specifies the number of remembered steps in the history of ionic convergence runs, or the number of ionic displacements in frozen phonon calculations.
</p>
