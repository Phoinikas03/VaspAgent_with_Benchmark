<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">LUSE_VDW</a> = [logical]<br/> Default: <b>LUSE_VDW</b> = .FALSE. 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">LUSE_VDW</a>=.TRUE. switches on the use of a nonlocal vdW-DF functional. These functionals depend on the electron density at two points in space and model long-range van der Waals (dispersion) correlation effects.
</p>
