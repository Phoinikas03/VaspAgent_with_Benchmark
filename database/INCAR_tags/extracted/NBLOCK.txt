<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a> = [integer]<br/> Default: <b>NBLOCK</b> = 1 
</p>
<p>Description: After <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a> ionic steps the pair-correlation function and the DOS are calculated
and the ionic configuration is written to the <a href="/wiki/index.php/XDATCAR" title="XDATCAR">XDATCAR</a>-file.
</p>
<p>It is recommended to leave  <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a> to 1, since the computational overhead to determine the DOS and pair correlation function is minimal. Only for  molecular dynamics simulations with many 1000 steps or when using machine-learned force fields, it might be expedient to increase <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a> to say 10 or even 100, to avoid large <a href="/wiki/index.php/XDATCAR" title="XDATCAR">XDATCAR</a>-files and the evaluation of the pair correlation function at every step. 
</p>
<p>In addition
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a> controls how often the kinetic energy is scaled if <code style="padding: 2px"><a href="/wiki/index.php/SMASS" title="SMASS">SMASS</a> = -1</code>.</li></ul>
<ul><li>After <code><a href="/wiki/index.php/KBLOCK" title="KBLOCK">KBLOCK</a> * <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a></code> ionic steps the averaged pair correlation function and DOS are written to the files <a href="/wiki/index.php/PCDAT" title="PCDAT">PCDAT</a>  and <a href="/wiki/index.php/DOSCAR" title="DOSCAR">DOSCAR</a>. The internal accumulators are reset, and after another <code><a href="/wiki/index.php/KBLOCK" title="KBLOCK">KBLOCK</a> * <a class="mw-selflink selflink">NBLOCK</a></code> steps the new averaged quantities are written out.</li></ul>
