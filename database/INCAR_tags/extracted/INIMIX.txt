<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a> = 0 | 1 | 2<br/> Default: <b>INIMIX</b> = 1 
</p>
<p>Description: Determines the functional form of the initial mixing matrix in the Broyden scheme (<a href="/wiki/index.php/IMIX" title="IMIX">IMIX</a>=4).
</p>
<p>The initial mixing matrix might influence the convergence speed for complex situations (especially surfaces and magnetic systems), nevertheless <a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a> must not be changed from the default setting: anything which can be done with <a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a> can also be done with <a href="/wiki/index.php/AMIX" title="AMIX">AMIX</a> and <a href="/wiki/index.php/BMIX" title="BMIX">BMIX</a>, and changing <a href="/wiki/index.php/AMIX" title="AMIX">AMIX</a> and <a href="/wiki/index.php/BMIX" title="BMIX">BMIX</a> is definitely preferable.
</p>
<p>Possible choices for INIMIX are:
</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a>=0</li></ul>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a>=1</li></ul>
<ul><li><a class="mw-selflink selflink">INIMIX</a>=2</li></ul>
