<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">EMIN</a> = [real] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EMIN</a> specifies the  lower boundary of the energy range for the evaluation of the electronic <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/index.php/Density_of_states" title="Density of states">density of states</a> (DOS).
</p>
<p>The DOS is evaluated each <a href="/wiki/index.php/NBLOCK" title="NBLOCK">NBLOCK</a> steps, <a href="/wiki/index.php/DOSCAR" title="DOSCAR">DOSCAR</a> is updated each <a href="/wiki/index.php/NBLOCK" title="NBLOCK">NBLOCK</a>*<a href="/wiki/index.php/KBLOCK" title="KBLOCK">KBLOCK</a> steps. 
</p>
