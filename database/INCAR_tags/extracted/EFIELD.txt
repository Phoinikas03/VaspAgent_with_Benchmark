<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">EFIELD</a> = [real] 
</p>
<p>Description: <a class="mw-selflink selflink">EFIELD</a> controls the magnitude of the applied electric force field.
</p>
<p>It is possible to apply an external electrostatic field in slab, or molecular calculations. Presently only a single value can be supplied and the field is applied in the direction selected by <a href="/wiki/index.php/IDIPOL" title="IDIPOL">IDIPOL</a>=1-4. The electric force field is supplied in units of eV/Å. Dipole corrections to the potential (<a href="/wiki/index.php/LDIPOL" title="LDIPOL">LDIPOL</a>=.TRUE.) can and should be turned on to avoid interactions between the periodically repeated images.
</p>
